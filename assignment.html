Digital assest management systems: another type of CMS, which is instead used to manage content with clearly defined author or ownership.

Full-Stack Development Process

In my opinion, the full-stack development preocess can be seperated into four steps, identify a problem, develop a solution, test the solution, and update CI/CD.

These steps layout how you design and code around the solution for said problem and then intial, and then continous development and integration for either scaling, marketing, or sales.
